Dallin Hatch



Armed with only an iPhone and a dream, Dallin Hatch began writing his first songs at the age of 15. Fast forward to 21, Hatch has already captivated the hearts of millions of viewers and listeners on the ever popular app, TikTok. Hatch is known for his piercing and raw vocals. Originals like, "A Part of Me" or "The Internet" are just a few examples of his openness with the public eye. Hatch truly wears his heart on his sleeve. Now surpassing over 200,000 + streams with music made using only an iPhone, Hatch continues to put his energy into creating emotionally packed songs which is seen throughout his discography.
